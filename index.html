Hi! I'm Nicole

I'm a UX/UI Designer with a background in graphic design.

So, you want to know a bit more about me? It all started after I got my first digital camera. It was a 1.2MP Fujifilm Finepix camera. That’s right. One-point-two! That little camera came with Photoshop Elements and it changed my life.

Fast-forward a few years and you’ll find me in marketing departments designing all kinds of things that go to print or are displayed online for the world to see.

Like most life-long learners, I spent some time exploring ways to expand my design knowledge and skills. UX/UI seemed like a natural step, so I decided to enroll myself in the University of Oregon UX/UI Bootcamp. The growth wasn’t always comfortable, but I could feel in my bones that it was the path I needed to be on.

My design career has been rewarding, and it just keeps getting better.
